AIRLINE INCIDENTS

Aircraft Incidents. Nearly everyone who has flown on an aircraft has, at some point or another, experienced fear. What if something bad happens? What if the plane crashes? What if we fly into a storm? Most of us, after flying a few times, get over these fears and learn to put them aside - until we see an accident on the news. Most recently, in March 2019, Ethiopian Airlines Flight 302 crashed just minutes after takeoff, killing all 157 passengers. But is this kind of scenario common?
